Analytics cloud

Grow your business and boost your productivity with Salesforce Analytics cloud.

Get In Touch    Why Choose Us

At Cloud Certitude, we specialize in Analytics Cloud Salesforce implementation, empowering businesses to gain valuable insights from their data, make data-driven decisions, and drive business growth. Analytics Cloud, also known as Salesforce Einstein Analytics, is a powerful analytics platform offered by Salesforce that enables organizations to explore, visualize, and analyze data in a user-friendly and intuitive way.


We as a team at Cloud Certitude have been emphasizing and providing complete satisfaction to our clients globally since 2013.

Based on your requirements, we design a robust cloud architecture that ensures optimal performance, scalability, and reliability

Our Approach to Analytics Cloud Salesforce Implementation:

  • Requirement Analysis: We begin by understanding your specific analytics goals, data sources, and pain points. Our team of experts collaborates closely with you to gather insights and define the scope of the Analytics Cloud implementation. We analyze your existing data landscape to identify opportunities for data integration, transformation, and visualization.
  • Data Integration and Transformation: We optimize data integration and transformation within Analytics Cloud. Our team configures data connectors and pipelines to extract data from various sources such as customer relationship management (CRM) systems, marketing platforms, sales databases, and more. We cleanse and transform the data to ensure accuracy and consistency for meaningful analytics.
  • Data Modeling and Visualization: We design data models and create interactive visualizations within Analytics Cloud. Our experts create data sets, lenses, and dashboards that align with your business objectives. We enable you to explore data, slice and dice it, and gain actionable insights through visually appealing charts, graphs, and interactive dashboards.
