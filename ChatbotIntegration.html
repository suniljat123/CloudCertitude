A chatbot is a piece of software that allows customers to engage with your business and perform basic tasks or access certain information without the need for a human agent to interact with them.

Integrating a chat bot with Salesforce can be a powerful way to streamline your sales and customer service processes. By connecting your chat bot with Salesforce, you can automate data capture, lead generation, customer interactions, and more.

Integrating a chat bot with Salesforce can provide several benefits for your organization. Not only do chat bots give your customers access to your business 24 hours a day, but they are also able to receive immediate responses to some basic questions around the clock.

Organisations may automate procedures, boost productivity, and provide a better customer experience by utilizing the connection of a chat bot with Salesforce. It enables companies to enhance their sales and support operations while delivering individualised and rapid service.
