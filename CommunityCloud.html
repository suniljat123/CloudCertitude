Education Cloud

Grow your business and boost your productivity with Salesforce Education Cloud.

Get In Touch    Why Choose Us

Education Cloud is a platform built on Salesforce CRM aimed at the education sector, including schools through to higher education institutions like universities and colleges. This cloud is aimed at keeping students, alumni and staff connected.

Education Cloud has a few extra benefits such as the Power of Us hub, K-12 focus, K-20 focus and Education Data Architecture. All of these features enable Education Cloud to better connect students, staff, schools, districts and universities, as well as give schools and universities a 360-degree holistic view of the education journey.

We as a team at Cloud Certitude have been emphasizing and providing complete satisfaction to our clients globally since 2013.

Based on your requirements, we design a robust cloud architecture that ensures optimal performance, scalability, and reliability

Education Cloud and its features in the education sector

  • Power of Us Hub: A community support hub that offers Education Cloud users access to 10 free subscriptions and discounts on other Salesforce subscriptions, products, and services.
  • K-12: Refers to the school years from Kindergarten to 12th grade in high school. Education Cloud is designed to cater to K-12 institutions, providing a centralized platform for managing student data and personalizing interactions between the school, students, and their families.
  • K-20: Refers to the school years from Kindergarten to a college degree. Salesforce's Education Data Architecture allows both K-12 and Higher Ed institutions to create a holistic view of their students, enabling tracking of dual enrollment, learner profiles, and supporting lifelong learning.
