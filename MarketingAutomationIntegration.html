Some best practices to keep in mind when designing your marketing automation strategy

Describe and present your objectives. To convince your stakeholders to support the purchase of marketing automation software, present hard data.

Work with different teams. Several teams in your organisation will be impacted by your marketing automation approach. Before you start, get their opinion and buy-in.

Visualize the processes. Use thorough flowcharts of your marketing automation process to effectively and efficiently communicate your organization's overall goals.

Make sure your database is segmented. Think about your consumer information. Consider who you want to engage and why.
