Mass DML:-

 Mass DML empowers administrators to effortlessly update and delete records in a streamlined manner.

Mass DML is a robust Salesforce Update and Delete manager application designed to streamline and simplify the process of making bulk updates and deletions. With its advanced filtering capabilities, administrators can efficiently update and delete records in large quantities. The application provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to define specific criteria and conditions for selecting the records to be modified or removed. By leveraging Mass DML, organizations can save significant time and effort by performing bulk operations on their Salesforce data accurately and swiftly, resulting in improved data management and increased operational efficiency.

Mass DML is a powerful tool that empowers administrators to efficiently perform bulk update and delete actions. To initiate these actions, administrators create a job record within the tool. This job record serves as a container for the bulk operation, allowing administrators to define various parameters and customize the action according to their specific requirements.
